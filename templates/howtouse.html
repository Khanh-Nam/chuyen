How to Use

Welcome to the Student Management System. Below are the steps to effectively use this system:

  1. Registration: If you are a new user, please sign up by clicking on the "Sign Up" link on the login page. Fill in the required information and submit the form.
  2. Log In: After registering, log in with your email and password. If you forget your password, you can reset it using the "Reset Password" link.
  3. Manage Students: Once logged in, you can add, update, or remove student records. Navigate to the respective sections in the dashboard.
  4. Contact Support: If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out using the "Contact Us" page.
  5. Logout: Always remember to log out when you are done using the system for security purposes.
